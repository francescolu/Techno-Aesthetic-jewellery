## Techno-Aesthetic Mechanisms

This research defines techno-aesthetic mechanisms in jewellery as the interaction between form, technique, and production logic.

### 1. Form Mechanism
- Symmetry and geometry
- Ornament structure
- Proportion and rhythm

### 2. Technical Mechanism
- Material constraints
- Stone setting logic
- Fabrication processes

### 3. Production Mechanism
- Standardization
- Cost efficiency
- Mass production logic
