# Techno-Aesthetic Jewellery

## Research Overview
This repository documents my PhD research on the techno-aesthetic mechanisms of 20th-century commercial jewellery and explores how these mechanisms can be reconstructed or simulated through AI-assisted design.

The project bridges historical analysis, design practice, and computational experimentation, forming a research-driven design system.

---

## Research Question
What are the techno-aesthetic mechanisms underlying 20th-century commercial jewellery, and how can these mechanisms be reconstructed or simulated through AI-assisted design?

---

## Research Objectives
- To identify and define techno-aesthetic mechanisms in commercial jewellery
- To analyze historical jewellery (Art Nouveau, Art Deco, Modernism)
- To extract form, material, and production logic
- To reconstruct these mechanisms using AI-based design workflows
- To evaluate results through aesthetic and manufacturability criteria

---

## Theoretical Framework

### Techno-Aesthetic Mechanisms
This research conceptualizes jewellery design as the result of interacting mechanisms:

1. **Form Mechanism**
   - Proportion, symmetry, rhythm, ornament logic

2. **Technical Mechanism**
   - Material constraints (thickness, hardness)
   - Craft and fabrication processes (casting, setting)

3. **Production Mechanism**
   - Mass production vs handcrafted logic
   - Cost, scalability, and standardization

---

## Research Workflow
